    New Build properties with New Approach

    All New Build developments with third party backed 10 year warranty and 21st century interiors.
    At "ScotRuss Builders" New Build Construction Company we excel at delivering the New Build projects, flats and houses, whilst primarily focusing on the high specification residential developments.

    Our expertise track record and and national construction trade body memberships are assuring we will meet and exceed the requirements of new build planners and building control bodies. Our aim is to carry on building on our track record of the new build properties that are not only meeting the minimum new build standards but also exceeding the client expectations in detail quality and construction process easiness with our inhouse project management service.

    We are keen to use the latest construction materials and processes, where fits the surrounding environment, so to produce the stylish and luxurious properties for you to call a home.

    Our company is a member of the several constructor schemes who monitor our construction processes and impartially verify our high quality construction service delivers to the industry standards at all times.

    Our main source of new build custom comes from different Glasgow areas, Troon, Air property developments whereby the potential parcels of land on the side or back of the existing properties are turned into the separate development plots for the new build. However, we have also worked on the redevelopment projects whereby the existing structure had to be knocked down in order to be replaced by the new build property.

    Architectural services

    If your project requires planning or building warrant advise we would recommend discussing this with a register Architect. We work in partnership with A10 Architects Ltd delivering various projects from new builds, house extensions, loft conversions and refurbishments where A10 Architects Ltd can provide simple advise to full contract administration to assist in the completion of your project.

    The most common Home Improvements

    The most common home improvement is getting a new bathroom (39%), closely followed by installing a kitchen (38%). It asked about 2000 homeowners (in March 2018) what home improvements they have made in the past five years. Also in the top 10 list were:

    • Installing a new boiler or central heating system (34%)


    • Having a garden make-over including building garden room or garden office (26%)


    • Installing double glazing (26%)


    • Building an extension (17%)


    • Knocking through rooms (12%)


    • Getting a loft & garage conversion (10%)


    • Adding an extra bedroom (9%)

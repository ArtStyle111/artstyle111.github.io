    Stretch Ceilings in Glasgow - Supply and Install

    ScotRuss Builders offers to supply and install Stretch Ceilings in Glasgow areas.

    ScotRuss Builders, are the Glasgow based independent Stretch Ceiling Company and now offer an independent extensive range of stretch ceilings.

    Stretch Ceiling, Suspended Ceilings, Ceiling Features offer the fantastic opportunity for added dimension in design and critically, ceiling performance. Stretch Ceilings are available in over 250 colours, finishes, textures and styles. Stretch Ceilings are fully certified, Non Toxic, Recyclable, Sustainable and suitable for use in Commercial and Domestic environments.

    ​ With ScotRuss Builders your stretch ceiling design options are pretty much unlimited.

    You can choose your colour, the finish, and the shape. Translucent ceilings are a brilliant choice if you want to light the ceiling from above. Printed ceilings can create a real impact for your home or business premises.

    Browse some example options below but please contact us to explore more design options.

    Colours and Finishes

    We offer a very wide wide range of stretch ceiling colours and finishes. Here are just a few of the options available. Please contact us to discover the full range.

    Gloss

    Gloss (or lacquer) ceilings are a popular choice for commercial premises such as showrooms, restaurants, swimming pools and bars. Gloss ceilings are great for making small rooms appear larger.

    Satin & Matt

    Matt is the most traditional finish and achieves a look comparable to a plastered ceiling. A satin finish adds a bit more gloss. Both matt and satin stretch ceilings are available in a range of pastel colours.

    Printed ceilings

    Create a genuine ‘wow’ factor with a printed stretch ceiling. We use eco solvent UV printing technologies to turn your favourite photo or illustration into an amazing ceiling. Printed ceilings look fantastic in the home but are also great for companies who want to brand their showroom or exhibition stands with their logos and identity.
